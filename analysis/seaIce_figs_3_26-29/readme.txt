
These scripts produce the sea ice figures for Structure and Performance of GFDL's CM4.0 Climate Model, DOI: 10.1029/2019MS001829

To produce all figures, simply run produceCM4_sea_ice.m, changing "work_dir" and "fig_dir" to the user's appropriate directories

These scripts produce Figures 3, 26, 27, 28, and 29 of the CM4.0 documentation paper.
